About Fahad

Fahad Shahbaz began Youth General Assembly at the age of eighteen with a clear belief: young Pakistanis deserve a real seat at the policy table.

Over the years, YGA has grown into a youth-led platform that brings together emerging voices, civic dialogue, and institutional engagement. Its work reflects a wider movement of young people asking not only to be heard, but to help shape the systems that affect their future.

“From one idea at eighteen to a platform for youth participation, Fahad Shahbaz’s work reflects the power of civic imagination.”

— Cosmopolitan Art Magazine Pakistan

Why This Matters

Cosmopolitan Art Magazine Pakistan features people who shape culture, creativity, and public imagination. Fahad’s story sits within that wider mission: highlighting individuals who are creating space for dialogue, representation, and change.

Through Youth General Assembly, his work focuses on youth leadership, civic engagement, and building bridges between young people and policy conversations.
