Submission Requirements

What We Need From You

To help our editorial team understand your story, please include clear details, strong images, and correct credits.

Send These Details

  • Full name, brand, gallery, or project name
  • Short bio or introduction
  • Artist statement or project note
  • Instagram, website, or portfolio links

Send These Assets

  • 4–8 high-resolution images
  • Image captions and credits
  • Exhibition/event dates if applicable
  • Contact number or email for follow-up

How It Works

From Submission to Feature

Step 01
