About Issue 01

A New Creative Archive

The inaugural issue of Cosmopolitan Art Magazine Pakistan marks the beginning of a platform dedicated to documenting Pakistan’s evolving creative landscape. From leading contemporary artists and emerging voices to exhibitions, food culture, architecture and design, Issue 01 brings together stories that deserve to be preserved and shared.

This issue is built as both a digital experience and a future printed collectible for artists, readers, galleries, universities and creative communities across Pakistan.
