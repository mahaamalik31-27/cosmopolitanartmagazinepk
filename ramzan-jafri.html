Featured Artist

Ramzan
Jafri

Miniature Painting · Contemporary Book Art · NCA Lahore

Ramzan Jafri is a contemporary Pakistani artist whose practice bridges Mughal miniature traditions with contemporary visual storytelling. Through illuminated book structures, symbolic imagery, and deeply reflective compositions, his work explores memory, devotion, artistic inheritance, and the timeless relationship between knowledge and creativity.

@qalmkarramzanjafri

The Poetry of Love

Created on a sculptural book cover, The Poetry of Love brings together tradition, artistic devotion, and contemporary reflection. The work features Ramzan Jafri alongside Abul Hasan — the legendary Mughal miniature painter — creating a visual dialogue between historical mastery and present-day artistic practice.

The illuminated spine references traditional manuscript embellishment, connecting the piece to the aesthetics of historical Islamic book art. Through self-portraiture, symbolism, and intricate craftsmanship, the work reflects on the perseverance, discipline, and emotional labor embedded within every artist’s journey.

“Art becomes timeless when memory, devotion, and tradition speak through the same surface.”

— Ramzan Jafri

The piece ultimately becomes a meditation on memory, artistic inheritance, and the enduring spiritual connection between artist and art across generations.
