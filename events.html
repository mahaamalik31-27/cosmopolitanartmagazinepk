Gadrung DHA Lahore

Where Hyderabad Deccan cuisine meets miniature art, memory, heritage, and culinary storytelling.

Food Culture · Lahore

Gadrung:
Art on a Plate

Hyderabadi Cuisine · DHA Lahore · Founded by Shireen Bano Rizvi

Gadrung is a Hyderabad Deccani restaurant in DHA Lahore founded by visual artist, miniature painter, and chef Shireen Bano Rizvi. Rooted in family recipes, pure ingredients, and artistic memory, Gadrung brings traditional Deccan flavors to Lahore through food prepared with mustard oil, desi ghee, authentic spices, and a deeply personal cultural vision.
