Gadrung:
Hyderabad Deccani Cuisine

Founded by artist and chef Shireen Bano Rizvi, Gadrung brings the authentic flavours of Hyderabad Deccan to Lahore through traditional recipes, pure ingredients and a deeply personal cultural vision rooted in art, memory and heritage.

ATALVI Gelato:
Italian Craft in Lahore

ATALVI Gelato brings a handcrafted Italian-style gelato experience to Main Gulberg, Lahore. Built around founder Omer’s journey from a memorable scoop in London to creating authentic gelato in Pakistan, the brand celebrates real flavour, texture and craft.

From Gelato University training to carefully prepared flavours like Pistachio Kunafa, Crème Brûlée, Stracciatella and Belgian Chocolate, ATALVI feels like a refined dessert experience shaped by intention and quality.

Art Exhibition

Fugitive Bodies,
Speculative Borderlands

Curated by Abdullah Qureshi and presented at Olomopolo, Lahore, Fugitive Bodies, Speculative Borderlands brings together works by Tamara Al-Mashouk, Sara Khan, Syowia Kyambi, Sandeep Johal and Risham Hosain Syed.

A quiet but powerful exhibition on memory, movement, archives, migration, identity and the politics of belonging, it invites viewers to see the borderland not only as rupture, but also as imagination, resistance and transformation.

Art Exhibition

Muted
Dialogues

Presented at Ejaz Art Gallery, Muted Dialogues brings together a compelling group of contemporary artists whose practices explore silence, restraint, perception and the expressive power of minimal form.

Featuring works by Arif Hussain Khokhar, Bazil Habib, Faheem Abbas, Faizan Riedinger, Noshad Batoti, Sabir Ali Talpur, Sajid Ali and Sara Riaz Khan, the exhibition creates a thoughtful dialogue between stillness and presence.

Art Exhibition

Ummeed e Bahar
A Ray of Hope

Presented at Taseer Art Gallery, Lahore, Ummeed e Bahar is a thoughtfully curated exhibition exploring hope, landscape, memory, resilience and humanity's enduring relationship with nature.

The exhibition brings together Irfan Channa, Kaleem Khan, Nasir Shehzad, Rabbia Khalid Khan, Raja Najam ul Hassan, Sajjad Nawaz and Yawar Abbas in a poetic visual dialogue around renewal and the quiet strength of place.
