Featured Artist · Origami · Mixed Media

Saba
Haroon

Origami · Mixed Media · Lahore, Pakistan

Born in Jeddah, shaped by Lahore — Saba Haroon folds hundreds of origami cranes into sweeping formations that carry the weight of emotion, memory, and the quiet beauty of impermanence.

"I fold cranes as quiet extensions of myself. Each delicate fold holds time, patience, and a sense of presence."
