Cover Feature · Issue 01
Saba
Haroon
Origami · Mixed Media · Lahore, Pakistan
Guided by Wabi-Sabi — the beauty of impermanence and imperfection — Saba Haroon folds origami cranes as quiet extensions of herself, creating meditative installations that speak of stillness, memory, and emotion.
“I fold cranes as quiet extensions of myself. Each delicate fold holds time, patience, and a sense of presence.”
