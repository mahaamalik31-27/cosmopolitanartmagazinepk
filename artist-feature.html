Issue 01 · Featured Artists

Contemporary
Voices

Exploring contemporary Pakistani art through installation, miniature painting, figurative practice, visual storytelling, memory, symbolism, abstraction, and emotional narratives shaping South Asian creative culture.

Featured Artist 01

Saba
Haroon

Origami · Mixed Media · Lahore, Pakistan

Guided by Wabi-Sabi, the beauty of impermanence and imperfection, Saba Haroon creates meditative installations inspired by healing, memory, symbolism, and emotional transformation through folded forms and spatial storytelling.

Featured Artist 02

Ramzan
Jafri

Miniature Painting · Contemporary Book Art · NCA Lahore

Ramzan Jafri bridges Mughal miniature traditions with contemporary visual storytelling through illuminated book structures, symbolic imagery, manuscript aesthetics, and reflections on artistic inheritance and devotion.

Read Feature

Featured Artist 03

Saqiba
Suleman

Figurative Painting · Magical Realism · Pakistan

Saqiba Suleman explores the relationship between human figures, floral symbolism, sensory memory, and visual pleasure through richly layered compositions where reality and imagination merge into poetic forms of magical realism.

Read Feature

Featured Artist 04

Sana
Dar

Abstract Expressionism · Acrylic · Collage · Islamabad

Sana Dar’s work moves through colour, instinct, contrast, and emotional intensity. Working across acrylic, ink, emulsion paint, collage, and hand cut paper, she builds abstract worlds that explore duality, memory, emotion, and the harmony between opposing forces.
